METHOD STUDY STYLE REFERENCE

Use concise industrial action descriptions like these:

1. Move and hold fabric to make two edges align with both hand.
2. Put fabric on bed for sewing with both hand.
3. Move fabric forward to set inside feed dog with left hand. Right hand moves backward to hold fabric alignment.
4. Push fabric forward under needle (sew) with left hand. Right hand moves simultaneously with fabric grab.
5. Left hand releases fabric after passing by feed dog. Right hand holds fabric end. Operator reaches fabric edge for further sewing with both hand.
6. Operator re-aligns fabric edges with both hand.
7. Bare left hand moves from dispatch zone to get fabric zone. Bare right hand reaches accessories zone after that.
8. Move fabric from get zone to dispatch zone by left hand. Right hand reaches accessories zone.
9. Disengage shoulder tape from bundle and move it towards machine bed by both hand.
10. Attach shoulder tape with machine. Right hand spins it with machine part and left hand holds one end of shoulder tape in feed dog.
